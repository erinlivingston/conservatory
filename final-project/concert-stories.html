Concert Stories

In 2025 I began a project of collecting data for all concerts I recall attending through my life. From this data I created a series of visualizations showing my attendance history, but in the process, I felt the stories behind my experiences were lost. This is my attempt to retroactively collect stories, pictures, and videos and create a journal for some of the most memorable events I attended between 2000 and 2025.
